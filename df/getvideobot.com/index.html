About Us

@GetVideoBot is a Twitter bot made to help you download videos & gifs from twitter.

Mention @GetVideoBot in a reply to any tweet with a video or gif, and you will get a response in few seconds.

Alternatively, you can copy and paste the link of the tweet on the text field above and we will extract the video download link and present it to you in different MP4 formats.

How to Download Twitter Videos?

You can download videos from Twitter by mentioning @GetVideoBot ina reply to a video or gif tweet.

If you'd like to use our website directly, these are the steps to download videos from Twitter:

  • 1. Find the Tweet that has the video you want download.
  • 2. Tap the share icon and copy the tweet link.
  • 3. Paste the tweet link into the URL text box above and click on "Download" button.
  • 4. Select and download the MP4 format of your choice to your device.
